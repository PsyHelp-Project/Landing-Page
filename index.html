About PsyHelp

Our application is designed to empower clinics to provide a more personalized and accessible service to their patients

We understand the importance of fostering a close relationship between clinicians and individuals seeking mental health support.

Connection with the patients

With PsyHelp, clinics can seamlessly connect with their patients, offering a comprehensive range of mental health resources and tools. From scheduling appointments and securely sharing assessments to providing targeted interventions and progress tracking.

Unique experience

PsyHelp facilitates a more intimate and tailored experience for patients. Our goal is to enhance the therapeutic journey by promoting consistent communication, empowering patients to actively participate in their mental wellness, and ultimately fostering positive outcomes.

We are one

Join us in revolutionizing the way clinics deliver mental health care. Together, let's create a nurturing and supportive environment for patients to thrive.

Services

Our app offers you the convenience and peace of mind of having all these features integrated in one place, so you can focus on your well-being and receive the best support possible

Appointment Scheduling

PsyHelp allows you to schedule appointments quickly and effortlessly. You can choose the date and time that works best for you, and you'll receive automatic reminders to ensure you never miss an important appointment.

Patient Tracking

We value the importance of continuous and personalized monitoring in your treatment journey. Our app enables you to keep a detailed record of your past sessions, making it easy to review and analyze your progress over time.

Prescription Management

Our app provides an integrated system to review your current medications and receive reminders to take them. Additionally, your medical prescriptions can be securely uploaded and stored in the app, allowing you to access them easily whenever needed.
